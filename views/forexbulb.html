Ideas
The motivation for creating this site is to generate forex ideas. Much of what this site is going to contain will be related to trading strategies, data analysis and validation techniques.

There are three ideas that are to be thoroughly developed in the near future. These are Simple Moving Average Crossovers, Artificial Neural Networks and Genetic Algorithms. In order to accomplish these types of analysis, data from several currency pairs will be fed to this page and it will be rendered with the use of visualization techniques.

Creative visualizations are a crucial component for both strategy discovery and opportunity communication; in other words, visualization techniques can be useful to portray what to focus on to increase the likelihood of making a profit and what are the specif steps to achieve that.

  • Establish the main areas of discussion.
